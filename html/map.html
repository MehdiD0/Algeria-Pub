Algeria in the world

It is the biggest country lining the Mediterranean Sea and the vaster first one of Africa. It shares ground borders in the northeast with Tunisia, in the East with Libya, in the South with Niger and Mali, in the southwest with Mauritania and territory of the Western Sahara, and on the West with Morocco.

Algeria in Africa

Algeria is the biggest country in Africa and the tenth biggest in the world. It is located in the North of the continent, between Morocco and Tunisia. It is bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the North, the Western Sahara and Mauritania to the South, Libya and Tunisia to the East, and Morocco to the West.

Algeria from the inside

Their is 58 wilayas in Algeria, each one has its own culture and traditions. The capital of Algeria is Algiers, it is located in the north of the country. The official language is Arabic, but the most spoken language is the Algerian dialect. The currency is the Algerian Dinar (DZD).
